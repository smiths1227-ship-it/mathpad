// the idea

most math tools just give you an answer. punch in a problem, get a number, done. that's useless if you're actually trying to learn or check your work.

mathpad is different. every tool shows you the full working — every step laid out the way you'd write it on paper or a whiteboard. you can see exactly what happened and why.

built by zecarrot, 11th grader, as a side project. pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. no libraries, no backend, no accounts needed.

// what's included
  • arithmetic — long addition, subtraction, multiplication, division with carried digits
  • algebra — linear equation solver showing every operation applied
  • fractions — full fraction arithmetic with LCD steps
  • geometry — area and perimeter for 6 shapes with formula shown
  • exponents — powers, square roots, nth roots with expansion
  • pemdas — expression evaluator that highlights each operation in order
